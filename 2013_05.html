CULINARY ARCHITECTURE

Late 2012 brought the opening of three new restaurants designed by Heliotrope: Shanik, Joule and The Whale Wins. All three of these restaurants are owned by chefs at the top of their profession, and we worked diligently to keep our design solutions focused on their particular food and personalities. The resulting spaces are as different and unique as their owners. Since opening, all three were named semi-finalists for James Beard Awards, Shanik was featured in the New York Times, Joule and the Whale Wins were featured in April issue of Sunset Magazine (PDF) and Heliotrope was interviewed on restaurant design by the Daily Journal of Commerce.

MID-CENTURY TO MODERN

To remodel or re-build? That was the question pondered early in the design process for a recent project in Madison Park. The resulting house, which keeps the shell of the existing home and adds a new entry and garage while renovating the rest, was recently featured in the Wall Street Journal.
